Illustration: Sparrow

CHALLENGE: Illustrate an avatar representing an animal

Sparrow is one of the most widespread birds in the world. We may find them both in city and rural area. This tiny creature is fragile but tough.

This is a black-and-white illustration of a sparrow. In order to show this tiny animal is able to travel around the world, I chose a wing as a sign. In addition to the shape, I used the negative space to demonstrate the wing's layers.

This is a coloured illustration of a sparrow. I designed the background with leaves to show the relationship between the sparrow and nature. When I drew the sparrow, I accidentally discovered the sparrow looked like a superhero with a mask. Super Sparrow is born..

This is an illustration of Super Sparrow dancing with its shadow on the water. It is challenging to draw the shadow on water. I needed to keep the details of Super Sparrow while clearly showing it is only a shadow instead of a twin. So, I made the shadow transparent to some extent and added layers on top. In the meantime, I used soft colours and a childlike font to present the innocence and playful feeling.
